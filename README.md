**Classroom Zero**

Problem Statement Selected
Problem Statement 1: The Last-Minute Life Saver

Students frequently miss assignment deadlines because traditional reminder systems are passive and easy to ignore. While platforms like Google Classroom notify students about assignments, they do not proactively help students prioritize work, manage their workload, or create an effective study plan. This often results in last-minute submissions, increased stress, and reduced academic performance.

**Solution Overview**

Classroom Zero is an AI-powered academic productivity companion designed specifically for students using Google Classroom.
Instead of functioning as another reminder application, Classroom Zero acts as an intelligent study assistant. After connecting with Google Classroom, it automatically imports assignments, analyzes deadlines, estimates workload, identifies high-risk tasks, and proactively recommends what students should work on first.
Powered by Google Gemini, the application generates personalized study plans, predicts deadline risks, and provides actionable recommendations to help students complete their work before deadlines are missed.
The objective is to shift from passive notifications to proactive academic assistance.

**Key Features**

1. Google Classroom Integration
Securely connects with Google Classroom to automatically import courses, assignments, and deadlines into a unified dashboard.

2. AI Deadline Guardian
Continuously analyzes imported assignments to identify high-risk deadlines and proactively recommends which tasks should be prioritized.

3. Personalized AI Study Planner
Generates structured day-by-day study plans based on assignment descriptions, deadlines, and estimated workload using Gemini.

4. Emergency Mode
Provides an optimized recovery strategy for assignments approaching their deadlines, helping students maximize productivity under time constraints.

5. AI Academic Assistant
Allows students to interact with an AI assistant that provides assignment guidance, productivity recommendations, and study planning support.

6. Intelligent Dashboard
Displays assignment summaries, estimated study effort, workload analysis, and AI-generated daily recommendations in a single interface.

**Google Technologies Utilized**

Google AI Studio : Used as the primary AI development platform to design, build, and iterate the Classroom Zero application using Google's AI-powered app building capabilities.

Gemini API : Powers intelligent assignment analysis, personalized study plan generation, workload estimation, deadline risk prediction, and contextual academic recommendations.

Google Classroom API : Retrieves real-time classroom data, including courses, assignments, due dates, and coursework, enabling automatic synchronization and analysis.

Firebase : Provides backend services and supports application deployment and integration with Google's ecosystem.

**Project Impact**

Classroom Zero transforms Google Classroom from a passive assignment management platform into an intelligent academic companion that proactively helps students organize their workload, reduce stress, and meet deadlines more effectively through AI-driven planning and decision support.
